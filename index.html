GOAT online geneset enrichment analysis

Identify enriched genesets in a preranked genelist generated by e.g. proteomics or gene expression studies using the GOAT algorithm

GOAT algorithm features:

  • Sensitive: more significant genesets as compared to other methods
  • Accurate: geneset p-values are accurate under the null hypothesis
  • Fast: completes in seconds
  • No arbitrary parameters:
    unlike ORA, no fiddle parameters for ">k foreground genes" or "gene significance cutoff"
  • Bootstrapping approach always uses appropriate "background set":
    avoids all pitfalls commonly observed for ORA , as outlined by Wijesooriya et al. (2022)
  • Available as an R package and online data analysis tool (GOAT online in above menu)
*ORA: classical overrepresentation analysis (i.e. Fisher-exact or hypergeometric test)

The GOAT algorithm is described in the preprint manuscript;
Koopmans, F. (2023). GOAT: efficient and robust identification of geneset enrichment.
https://doi.org/10.1101/2023.12.10.570979
